German Pancakes

A German pancake (a.k.a. a Dutch baby) is similar to a large Yorkshire pudding. Unlike other types of pancakes, German pancakes are baked in the oven instead of fried on the stove. They also donâ€™t contain leaving ingredients, such as baking powder or baking soda.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Melt butter in a medium baking dish.

step 2

Mix flour, milk, eggs, and salt in a medium bowl. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish.

step 3

Bake on center rack in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes, until golden brown.
